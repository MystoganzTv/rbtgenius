## DESCRIPTION (4,000 chars max)
RBT Genius is the most comprehensive study app for the Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) exam, built around the official BACB RBT Task List 3rd Edition.

Whether you're studying for the first time or retaking the exam, RBT Genius gives you everything you need to pass with confidence.

──────────────────────────────────
WHAT'S INCLUDED
──────────────────────────────────

📚 1,100+ Practice Questions
Real exam-style questions covering all 6 domains of the RBT Task List:
• Measurement
• Assessment
• Skill Acquisition
• Behavior Reduction
• Documentation
• Professional Conduct & Ethics

Each question includes a detailed explanation so you understand the "why" behind every answer.

📋 85-Question Mock Exams
Simulate the real RBT exam with timed, full-length mock exams. Get a domain-by-domain breakdown of your performance after every exam, and track your readiness score over time.

🤖 AI Tutor
Ask any question about ABA concepts and get instant, exam-focused explanations. The AI Tutor helps you understand difficult topics at any hour — no tutoring sessions needed.

🃏 Flashcards
Reinforce key concepts with topic-filtered flashcard sessions. Mark cards as mastered and watch your deck shrink as your knowledge grows.

📊 Analytics & Progress Tracking
Your personalized readiness score updates with every answer. See your accuracy by domain, your weekly activity chart, study streak, and a complete history of your mock exams — all in one place.

🏆 Badges & Achievements
Earn badges as you hit milestones: answer your first 50 questions, keep a 3-day streak, ace a mock exam, and more.

🌐 English & Spanish
Full support for English and Spanish — switch at any time in Settings.
