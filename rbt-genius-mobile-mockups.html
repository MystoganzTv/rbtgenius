Duration recording
B

Why this is correct
Rate recording measures how often a behavior occurs in a given time period (count ÷ time). Recording per minute is the defining feature of rate measurement.

Measurement · A-1 Intermediate
An RBT records how many times a client engages in a target behavior per minute. Which measurement method is being used?
A
Duration recording
B
Rate recording
C
Latency recording
D
Momentary time sampling
The correct answer is B
Latency measures time from cue to behavior onset — not frequency per unit time. Rate divides count by time, so "per minute" is the key indicator.
